# Web Research

Research products and local services to find the best value options.

Read `agents/web-research/SKILL.md` and follow its workflow to help the user find the best products or services.

## How to Use

The user will ask about a product or service they want to research. Follow the workflow:

1. **Clarify** - Confirm what they're looking for and ask for location if it's a local service
2. **Discover Sources** - Find the most authoritative review sources for this category
3. **Research** - Search and fetch reviews from multiple trusted sources
4. **Synthesize** - Cross-reference findings and identify top options
5. **Deliver** - Present a best pick recommendation plus a ranked list with pros/cons and prices

## Output Format

Always provide:
- A **Best Pick** with clear justification
- A **Ranked List** (3-5 options) with pros, cons, and price ranges
- **Sources Consulted** with links

## Example Queries

- "Find me the best pressure washer under $400"
- "Best plumber near Austin, TX"
- "Top-rated robot vacuums for pet hair"
- "Reliable HVAC repair in Denver"
