# _inbox — raw capture

Fast, unshaped material lands here — especially **offline brain-dumps** when you
don't have time (or signal) to do a full interview.

## How to use
- Dump anything: a half-remembered story, a name, a date, a feeling. Don't polish it.
- Name files by date: `YYYY-MM-DD.md`, and timestamp entries inside.
- Later, ask the **Biographer** to "shape my inbox into stories" — it reads what's
  here, interviews you to fill gaps, and moves finished pieces to
  `Autobiography/Stories/`, then clears what it has processed.

## Example
```markdown
## 14:32
The summer we drove to Maine. The car broke down outside Portland. Dad and I...
(remember: the diner, the mechanic named Earl, mom was not amused)
```
