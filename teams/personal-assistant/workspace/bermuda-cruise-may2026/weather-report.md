## 4. Packing Call-Outs

### Sun / Beach (UV Very High in Bermuda)
- Wide-brim hat and quality polarized sunglasses
- Swimsuits, cover-ups, rash guard
- Walking shorts and lightweight tops
- Beach bag, flip-flops + walking shoes for Dockyard cobblestones

### Sea Days / Evenings
- Light sweater or fleece — evenings drop to ~60°F at sea
- Long pants for dinner / deck evenings
- Wind-resistant layer for open decks on rough sea days

### Rain Gear (Low priority)
- One compact packable rain jacket each — rain is low-probability in Bermuda but always possible

### Motion Comfort (especially May 18)
- Scopolamine patch (apply night before departure)
- Bonine / Dramamine Less Drowsy as backup
- Sea-Band wristbands
- Stay midship, lower decks on rough days

### Reef-Safe Sunscreen (Required in Bermuda)
Bermuda protects its coral reefs. **Avoid:** oxybenzone, octocrylene, octinoxate.
**Use instead:** mineral sunscreens with zinc oxide or titanium dioxide (Raw Elements, Thinksport, Badger, Blue Lizard Sensitive).
