Bottom Line

This is shaping up to be a solid cruise window. Bermuda is forecast dry and warm (76–80°F highs) for all three port days. Embarkation day (May 17) looks partly cloudy and mild ~70°F. No tropical threats — the Atlantic is quiet and stays that way through June 1. The main watch item is Atlantic swell: a cold front clearing the region around May 14–16 will leave residual 4–6 ft seas for the outbound crossing (May 18). By May 19 conditions should moderate. Pack sun protection (reef-safe), a light layer for sea days, and one compact rain layer as insurance.

4   Packing Call-Outs

Sun / Beach (Bermuda — Very High UV)

  • Wide-brim hat — UV 8–9 is no joke in May
  • Quality UV sunglasses (polarized for water glare)
  • Swimsuits, cover-ups, rash guard
  • Walking shorts + lightweight tops
  • Beach bag, flip-flops + walking shoes

Sea Days / Evenings

  • Light sweater or fleece — evenings drop to 60°F at sea
  • Long pants for dinner / cooler deck evenings
  • Wind-resistant layer for the bow on sea days
  • Comfortable walking shoes (non-slip)

Rain Gear (Low priority — pack light)

  • Compact packable rain jacket — one each
  • Rain is low probability in Bermuda but always possible
  • No need for umbrella — wind makes them useless

Motion Comfort (May 18 — moderate seas)

  • Scopolamine patch (Transderm Scop) — apply night before
  • Bonine / Dramamine Less Drowsy as backup
  • Sea-Band acupressure wristbands
  • Stay midship, lower decks on rough days
Reef-Safe Sunscreen — Required in Bermuda: Bermuda's coral reefs are protected under local regulations. Avoid sunscreens containing oxybenzone, octocrylene, or octinoxate — these chemicals are reef-toxic and some hotels/excursion operators will refuse entry if you're using them. Use mineral sunscreens with zinc oxide or titanium dioxide instead. Good options: Raw Elements, Thinksport, Badger, Blue Lizard Sensitive.
May 17 Sail-Away: Bring a camera for the Statue of Liberty passage (4:45–5:30 PM). Forecast shows SW winds and partly cloudy skies — good light, no rain expected. Dress in layers; it'll feel cool on an open deck moving at 18+ knots even at 70°F air temps.
