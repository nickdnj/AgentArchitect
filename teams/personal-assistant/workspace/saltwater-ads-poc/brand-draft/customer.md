# Saltwater Customer Profile — "Older Joe DeMarco"

**Source:** Joe DeMarco's email reply 2026-04-29 (he literally described the customer as "Older Joe DeMarco").
**Status:** POC draft.

## Demographics

- **Age:** 32-48
- **Gender:** Male (primary). Women's line exists (JD017 Cotton Knit Sweater, JD016 Split Test) — secondary volume.
- **Geography:** Long Island NY, Florida, New Jersey, Connecticut, Carolina beach markets — coastal suburbs broadly. Anywhere a coastal-lifestyle brand resonates.
- **Income:** Solid earner. Business owner, sales, finance, trades, real estate, hospitality.

## Personality

- Confident, easygoing, family-oriented, social
- Values quality over hype
- Wants to look put together without trying too hard
- NOT chasing trends — wants pieces that last

## Lifestyle

- Busy weekdays
- Gym, family time, golf, boat, dinners out, vacations
- Wants clothing that transitions through all of it
- Travels regularly (beach trips, golf trips, family vacations)

## Buying triggers (when he reaches for Saltwater)

- Before trips
- Golf outings
- Holidays (Memorial Day, July 4, Valentines, etc.)
- Spring refresh
- Layering season changes (fall/spring)
- Events
- Sees a fresh drop he likes

## What he wears Saltwater with

(Inferred from ad imagery JD001-JD021)
- Khaki shorts / chinos in summer
- Jeans + button-down for weekend dinners
- Layered with quarter-zip or hoodie for fall/spring
- Saltwater straw lifeguard hat (US flag patch is the signature)

## What success looks like to him

- Looks put together effortlessly
- Comfortable from morning gym to evening dinner
- Doesn't feel like a uniform
- Feels part of the brand story (family, coastal, real)

## What he avoids

- "Fratty" or "trying too hard"
- Stiff, hot, wrinkled traditional menswear
- Trendy hypebeast pieces that won't last
- Generic / disposable feeling
- Brands that talk down to him

## Hooks that resonate with this customer

- Comfort claims with specificity (not just "comfortable" — *which* moments comfort matters)
- Family business / authenticity
- Coastal lifestyle aspiration (boat, beach, golf course)
- Real reviews from people who sound like him
- Limited drops tied to real seasonal moments (not fake urgency)

## Hooks that fall flat with this customer

- Aspirational luxury copy
- Streetwear / hypebeast voice
- Generic DTC fluff
- Anything that requires too much explanation before he gets it
