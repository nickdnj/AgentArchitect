## Visual brand DNA (from sampled static ads)

- **Format:** 9:16 vertical (Meta/IG/TikTok native)
- **Photography:** Outdoor, natural light, coastal/water/dock — never studio
- **Models:** Real-looking 20s-40s, casual-confident, never staged
- **Typography:** Big bold sans-serif, punchy
- **Color palette:** Navy + white + USA red, seasonal pink for Valentines, natural earth tones
- **Brand mark:** Stylized shark+saltwater logo (fish silhouette with brand text)
- **Slogans seen:** "Coastal Comfort™" (registered TM)
- **CTA pattern:** Single button, simple verbs ("SHOP NOW", "BOGO")
- **Social-proof variant:** Real review screenshots arranged as collage with product hero
- **Authenticity signal:** Family business, real couple (Joe + Veronica), recurring talent rotation
