## Cast

### DAD (locked from real photo)
Nick's father, ~1981, early 40s. **Slimmed ~40 lbs** from the late-80s reference (younger, leaner
1981 build). Warm rounded face, **brown plastic-frame glasses**, thinning dark hair with **grey at
the temples**, short. Wardrobe: plaid flannel or short-sleeve button shirt, sleeves often rolled.
Gentle, focused, tinkerer's intensity. Setting: smoky basement repair office, EPROM reader, TRS-80.
Sheets: `style-test/dad-storybook-charactersheet.png`, `dad-storybook-trs80.png`.

### TEEN NICK (locked from real photo, junior prom 1981)
Nick at 16–17, 1981. **Stocky / solidly built — a high-school football tackle/lineman build**, broad
shoulders, fuller face. Dark feathered early-80s hair. Casual basement clothes (NOT the prom vest).
**No cigarette, not smoking.** Wide-eyed wonder at the CRT. Sheets:
`style-test/teennick-storybook-charactersheet.png`, `teennick-storybook-at-crt.png` (= storyboard P23).

### IKEGAMI ENGINEER (invented — lock this pass)
Japanese engineer, ~30s, **Tokyo, 1981**. Neat black hair, black-frame glasses, white short-sleeve
dress shirt, slim dark tie, dark slacks. Quietly proud, a little playful — the kind of person who'd
hide a "congratulations" message for whoever cracks his code. Setting: tidy early-80s engineering
lab, oscilloscope, hand-soldered Donkey Kong board, fluorescent light.

### BOOTLEGGER (invented — lock this pass)
Arcade-board copyist, ~1981, dim backroom. ~30s–40s, rolled sleeves. Surrounded by stacked cloned
PCBs, a chip programmer, and a corded desk phone (the one he uses to call Tokyo).
**FACE IS NEVER SHOWN** (per Nick): always seen from behind / in silhouette / deep shadow. Nick's
father was himself a bootlegger who cracked these chips — the anonymity deliberately leaves open that
the caller could have been Dad. "We'll never know." The character sheet only guides build/wardrobe/
silhouette, not a recognizable face.

### MUSEUM VISITOR (invented — lock this pass)
The MAME enthusiast who walks into InfoAge present-day, ~30–40. Casual: retro-gaming graphic tee,
zip hoodie or open flannel, small backpack. Friendly, curious, a little geeky. Appears at P32 with
Docent Nick.
