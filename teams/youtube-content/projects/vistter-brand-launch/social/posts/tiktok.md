## General TikTok Notes

- **Best posting times:** 11 AM - 1 PM ET and 7 PM - 9 PM ET on weekdays; 9 AM - 12 PM ET on weekends
- **Hook in first 2 seconds:** The text overlay must grab immediately. "Land pirates" and "Storm Warriors" are the strongest hooks.
- **Sound:** Use the original narration audio from the video. TikTok rewards original audio.
- **Duration:** Keep under 45 seconds. TikTok rewards completion rate -- shorter is better for discoverability.
- **CTA:** "Link in bio" or "Full documentary in bio" -- never a URL in the caption (TikTok doesn't hyperlink URLs in captions).
- **Cross-post to Reels:** Every TikTok can be dual-posted to Instagram Reels with minor caption adjustments (add @ mentions, remove TikTok-specific hashtags).
