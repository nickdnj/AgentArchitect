## Strategy: Trailer-Style Hooks, Not Sequential Clips

Each short is a standalone viral hook — not a chapter excerpt. The goal is to create
FOMO that drives viewers to the full 7-minute video AND to the app. Each short needs:

1. A hook that stops the scroll in the first 2 seconds (price shock or counterintuitive claim)
2. A fast internal arc (setup → reveal → proof)
3. A CTA that drives to the full video or the app
4. Pop-style subtitles burned in (mandatory — 70% of mobile views are silent)

### Why Trailer-Style Wins
Sequential clips suffer from viewer decay — each clip is weaker than the previous
because returning viewers have already seen it. Trailer-style clips each tell a complete
micro-story, pulling in cold audiences who have never seen the main video.

Analytics from prior projects (Monmouth Beach, Seven Presidents) confirmed:
trailer-style shorts 2-3x outperform sequential clips on reach and CTR.
