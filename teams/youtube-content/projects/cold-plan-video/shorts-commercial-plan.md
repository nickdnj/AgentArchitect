# Cold Plan — YouTube Shorts Commercial Series

## Concept: "The Generic Truth"

Purpose-built short-form commercials (30-45 sec each) promoting cold-plan-app.web.app. Not chopped from long-form — each is a standalone mini ad.

## Series 1: "What's REALLY In..." (The Reveal)

The hook: everyone knows brand names, nobody knows what's actually inside. Each short deconstructs one brand-name product into its generic pills with a price comparison.

| # | Title | Brand | Generic Recipe | Price Delta |
|---|-------|-------|---------------|-------------|
| 1 | What's REALLY in NyQuil? | NyQuil Cold & Flu | Acetaminophen + Doxylamine + DXM = 3 pills | ~$12 vs ~$0.47 |
| 2 | What's REALLY in DayQuil? | DayQuil Severe | Acetaminophen + Phenylephrine + DXM = 3 pills | ~$12 vs ~$0.45 |
| 3 | What's REALLY in Advil PM? | Advil PM | Ibuprofen + Diphenhydramine = 2 pills | ~$10 vs ~$0.30 |
| 4 | What's REALLY in Mucinex DM? | Mucinex DM | Guaifenesin + DXM = 2 pills | ~$15 vs ~$0.35 |
| 5 | What's REALLY in Tylenol PM? | Tylenol PM | Acetaminophen + Diphenhydramine = 2 pills | ~$11 vs ~$0.25 |

**Visual structure:**
1. HOOK (0-2s): Bold text "What's REALLY in NyQuil?" over dramatic image of the brand box
2. REVEAL (3-15s): Animated breakdown — brand box "explodes" into individual generic pills, each labeled
3. THE MATH (16-25s): Split screen — brand price vs generic price, with markup percentage
4. CTA (26-35s): "Build your own kit at coldplan.app" + QR code or URL

**Visual style:** Clean pharmaceutical aesthetic. White/blue palette. Bold sans-serif text overlays. GPT-image-1 for pill imagery, medicine cabinets, brand boxes.

## Production Notes

- **Image generation:** GPT-image-1 (gpt-4o image) via MCP — NO DALL-E
- **Narration:** Kokoro TTS or re-record key lines
- **Music:** Royalty-free, upbeat/confident tone
- **Format:** 9:16 vertical, 1080x1920
- **Length:** 30-45 seconds each
- **CTA:** Every short ends with coldplan.app URL
- **Hashtags:** #coldmedicine #generics #savemoney #pharmacy #lifehack #coldseason

## Recommended Launch Order

1. "What's REALLY in NyQuil?" (Series 1) — strongest hook, most recognizable brand
2. "The $0.47 NyQuil recipe" (Series 4) — controversial, drives engagement
3. "Sore Throat + Congestion" (Series 2) — relatable, practical
4. "My $18 Travel Cold Kit" (Series 3) — lifestyle appeal
5. Then alternate between series to keep it fresh

## Upload Cadence

- 2 shorts per week during cold season buildup (Sept-Nov)
- 3-4 per week during peak cold season (Dec-Feb)
- 1 per week off-season (Mar-Aug) — but start NOW to build the library and algorithm momentum before the Amazon 180-day deadline (~Oct 10)
