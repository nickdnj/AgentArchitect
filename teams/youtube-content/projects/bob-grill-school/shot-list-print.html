✅ Before you roll

  • AUDIO: iPhone in Bob's (button-down) chest pocket recording Voice Memos = primary voice track. Set Voice Memos to Lossless; phone in Airplane Mode + Do Not Disturb. Pocket fabric is the windscreen — no lav/deadcat needed. Camera's onboard mic = scratch track for syncing.
  • CLAP once at the head of EVERY take (Bob claps on camera) so the Voice Memo snaps to the video in post — non-negotiable.
  • Grab 2–3 min of clean ambient shore/gull sound separately for the audio bed
  • Tripod — lock it off for all demo/insert shots; handheld only for hooks & to-camera CTAs
  • Chimney starter, 2+ bags charcoal briquettes, newspaper, long lighter/matches, long tongs, spatula, heat gloves
  • Can of lighter fluid (Ep 4 only) + instant-read thermometer (Ep 3, must show 160°F clearly)
  • Test food: burgers (80/20, pre-formed or form on site), good hot dogs, buns, cheese, plate (Ep 3)
  • Reflector OR shoot golden hour to avoid raccoon-eye shadows; confirm 10 ft clearance per picnic rules
  • Heat gloves + sand bucket/water within reach; never leave coals unattended (Wharfside rule)

🔥 Shooting order

A charcoal fire has a one-way lifecycle (unlit → lighting → gray-ready → cooking → cooldown), so plan around 3 fires, not 5 relights. CRITICAL: you cannot demo lighter fluid (Ep 4) on coals already lit by a chimney (Ep 1), and the wind-lighting (Ep 5) needs an unlit firebox too. FIRE 1 (chimney): shoot Ep 1 lighting through gray-ready, then ride that same fire straight into Ep 2 readiness/hand-test/two-zone AND Ep 3 burgers & dogs, AND grab Ep 5's 're-spin during cook' beat over it — one fire covers Blocks A(Ep1)/B(Ep1)/C/D. FIRE 2 (lighter fluid, fresh unlit grill): shoot all of Ep 4 (Block A fluid-on-unlit → Block B side-light → safety beat). FIRE 3 (wind, fresh unlit grill — ideally a genuinely breezy moment): shoot Ep 5's back-to-wind setup and wind-blocked lighting. Batch ALL to-camera hooks/CTAs (Block E) and B-roll/end cards (Block F) separately — no fire required — so a flat shot doesn't cost you a fire. Net: 3 fires, ~2 relights. Tip: light Fire 2 and Fire 3 at adjacent grills back-to-back to parallelize.
