## Narration Script (trailer cut)

> This beach is named after seven presidents.
>
> The real number? Maybe four.
>
> Ulysses Grant came first. He held cabinet meetings on the porch and played poker with America's wealthiest men every Friday night.
>
> Then Garfield was shot. The bullet missed every vital organ. It was the infections that killed him.
>
> Volunteers laid thirty-two hundred feet of track overnight, by torchlight, to bring a dying president to the sea. The Elberon Hotel sent sandwiches.
>
> Woodrow Wilson ran his entire campaign from a fifty-two-room beach house. His slogan: He Kept Us Out of War. Five months later, he asked Congress to declare war on Germany.
>
> Three of the seven? Almost certainly fiction. But the stories they left behind are real.
>
> Full documentary. Link in description.

## Production Notes

### Vertical-native image handling

Each image is processed individually for 9:16:
- **Portrait images** (grant-portrait, garfield-portrait, wilson-portrait, hayes-portrait): Scale to fill 1080x1920, gentle zoom. These look GREAT in vertical because they're already tall/narrow.
- **Landscape images** (park scenes, cottage, Shadow Lawn, railroad): Scale height to 1920, crop width to 1080 from center. Use Ken Burns pan to reveal more of the scene over time.
- **ONE president per frame** — never show composites or multi-portrait shots.

Only 3 text overlay moments:
