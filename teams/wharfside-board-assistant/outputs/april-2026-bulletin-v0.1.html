Power Washing — Action Required

All buildings, patios, and balconies will be power and soft washed on Saturday, April 11 and Sunday, April 12. Buildings 1–4 will be washed on Saturday, and Buildings 5–8 on Sunday.

What You Must Do Before Your Building's Day:

  • Close ALL windows and balcony doors during the work.
  • Remove ALL items from your patio or balcony before work begins. This includes furniture, planters, grills, decorations — everything.
  • The vendor will NOT clean balconies or patios with items left on them and will NOT be responsible for damage to any items left behind.
  • The contractor will NOT return to complete any balconies or patios that were not cleared.
  • If you have renters, it is your responsibility to inform them of these requirements.

Please plan ahead and have your balcony or patio completely cleared the evening before your building's scheduled day. This ensures the work can be completed efficiently and protects your belongings.

Heating Infrastructure Upgrade

The community-wide boiler and piping replacement project continues to advance. Our engineering firm has completed a full review of all contractor bids, and the Board is actively evaluating proposals. This is a multi-phase project to modernize heating systems across all eight buildings — one of the most significant infrastructure investments in Wharfside Manor's history.

The Board is committed to making a well-informed decision and will communicate next steps as they are determined.

2026–2027 Insurance Renewal Milestone

We are pleased to announce that the Association has completed its insurance renewal for the 2026–2027 policy year — and the results are outstanding.

Approximately $200,000 in Annual Savings

After a thorough review of our options, the Board voted unanimously to switch the Association's insurance to World Insurance, resulting in approximately $200,000 in savings compared to the prior year's premium — with equivalent coverage from A/A+ rated carriers. We commend Property Manager Kathy Vanecek for her diligent work in securing these savings on behalf of the community.

New insurance certificates have been posted on the owner portal and distributed via email. If you need a copy for your mortgage lender or records, please log in to the portal or contact ECI.

Reminder: HO-6 Insurance Requirement

All unit owners are required to carry a Condominium Unit Owner's Policy (HO-6) with coverage sufficient to address the Association's $50,000 deductible. The winter pipe events were a stark reminder of how critical this coverage is. Declaration pages must be provided to management annually, and the Association must be named as additionally insured on all HO-6 policies. If your policy has lapsed or you are uncertain about your coverage level, please contact your insurance agent right away.

Fob Access System Expanding New

The Board is expanding our fob access system to additional common areas. This is the same fob system successfully used at the pool last summer. We are now updating the fob readers for the laundry rooms and Gym, bringing consistent security and access control across all shared amenities.

Fob Registration Required

All fobs must be registered as part of the Wharfside Census. If your fob does not work at any of the new readers, please contact ECI at 732-970-6886 to ensure your census information is up to date.

Temporary Laundry Room Access Disruption

While the new fob readers are being installed, there may be temporary interruptions to laundry room access. We apologize for any inconvenience and thank you for your patience as we work through this transition.

Community Reminders

Power Washing — April 11 & 12: As detailed above, all items must be removed from balconies and patios before your building's scheduled day. Windows and doors must be closed. This applies to owners and renters alike.

HO-6 Insurance: All unit owners are required to carry a Condominium Unit Owner's Policy (HO-6) with coverage sufficient to address the Association's $50,000 deductible. If you are unsure about your coverage, contact your insurance agent now.

Common Area Storage: Personal belongings must be kept in your assigned storage area only. Hallways, stairwells, and common areas must remain clear at all times for fire safety and emergency egress. The Board will continue to remove unauthorized items found in common areas.

Pet Policy: Pets must be on a leash at all times. Pets are not permitted on the grass, in flower beds, or in common areas. Please clean up after your pets promptly.

Parking: All vehicles must display a valid parking permit and be parked in the designated space shown on your permit. Visitor parking is limited — please use designated visitor spaces only.

Contacting the Board: Please direct all questions, maintenance requests, and concerns to ECI at 732-970-6886 or through the ECI work order system. Board members are volunteers and appreciate your understanding in routing inquiries through management.

Move-In / Move-Out Policy: The move-in/move-out deposit resolution is being finalized. Full details will be communicated to residents when complete.
