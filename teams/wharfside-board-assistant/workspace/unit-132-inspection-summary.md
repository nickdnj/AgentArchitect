## Gray zone — responsibility-for-cause vs. responsibility-for-finish
- Interior **mold/water damage** (under sink, kitchen, walls, ceilings) is the owner's to repair as *finish* — **but if the moisture source is a common element** (roof leak, HOA boiler/pipe), the Association owns fixing the *source*. Worth stating this distinction to the owner/buyer.
- **Water entry:** attic/roof leak → Association; window-/door-gap water → owner.
