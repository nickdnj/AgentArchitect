# Wharfside Crawl Spaces — Factual History & Board Next Steps

**Prepared:** 2026-05-25 · **Prompted by:** Linda Masessa's 5/23/26 email asking whether the Aug–Sep 2025 crawl space inspection items were ever addressed · **Status:** working draft for board review

---

## 1. The honest answer to Linda
The crawl spaces under all 8 buildings were inspected by ECI (Mike) between **8/21 and 9/22/2025**. Those reports — the ones Linda found on the portal — are **observation logs only: photos with one-line captions, no recommendations, no costs, no priorities.** The board acted on the urgent pieces (emergency mold remediation; obtained a legal opinion on responsibility; collected insulation estimates) but **never converted the reports into a single tracked, prioritized remediation plan.** The effort stalled in spring 2026. Picking it back up now is the right call.
