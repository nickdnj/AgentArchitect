If you're willing to help lead it, you'll have full support: the history, the tracker, and the ECI coordination, so you're not starting from scratch. If you'd rather not, no problem at all — either way, as a board we need to make sure it doesn't stall again.

I'll get it on the next agenda so we can take it up together. The two work orders you submitted for your own unit are folded into this. Thanks again for the push.
