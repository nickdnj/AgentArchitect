WHARFSIDE MANOR CONDOMINIUM ASSOCIATION

Resolution Establishing the Rate of Interest to be Charged on Outstanding Balances

POLICY RESOLUTION NO. 2019 - 1

WHEREAS, Section 10, of the Declaration of Restrictive and Protective Covenants, provides that: "every owner of a Unit shall automatically, upon becoming the owner of such unit, be a member of Association…"; and

WHEREAS, Section 16, of the Declaration of Restrictive and Protective Covenants, provides that: "each owner, tenant and occupant of a Unit shall comply with the provisions of this instrument and the By-Laws and Rules and Regulations of ASSOCIATION and failure to comply therewith shall be grounds for an action to recover sums due…"; and

WHEREAS, Section 18, of the Declaration of Restrictive and Protective Covenants, provides that: "the owner or co-owners of each unit are bound to contribute to the Common Expense of administration and of maintenance, repair or replacement of the Common Elements and the expenses of administering and maintain ASSOCIATION…"; and

WHEREAS, Article VI, Section 1, of the By-Laws provides that: "The affairs of the Association shall be governed by a Board of Trustees…"; and

WHEREAS, Article VI, Section 14, of the By-Laws provides that: "The Board of Trustees shall have and exercise all lawful powers and duties necessary for proper conduct and administration of the affairs of the Association and the operation and maintenance of the Condominium…."; and

WHEREAS, Section 8, of the By-Laws provides that: "A member shall be deemed to shall be deemed to be in "good standing" and "entitled to vote"…if, and only if, he shall have fully paid all assessments made or levied against him and his unit …together with interest, costs…"; and

WHEREAS, the Board of Trustees has determined it beneficial to expound on the language contained in Section 16 of the Declaration of Restrictive and Protective Covenants and provide a definition for the otherwise undefined term "sums due" as used therein; additionally, the Board of Trustees has determined it beneficial to provide the rate of interest that is charged to unit owners' outstanding balances with the Association as provided for in Section 8 of the Association’s By-Laws;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED THAT the Board of Trustees declares that Section 16 of the Declaration of Restrictive and Protective Covenants and Section 8 of the By-Laws, as set forth above, mean the following:

"Sums due" is a Unit Owner’s total outstanding balance with the Association, including any interest charged on that balance, as well as all unpaid assessments, fees and charges authorized by the Association’s Declaration of Restrictive and Protective Covenants; and
