WHEREAS, Grantor, by Master Deed and Declaration of Restrictive and Protective Covenants dated April 13, 1983 and recorded on April 18, 1983 in the Registrar of Deeds' Office of Monmouth County in Book 4401 of Deeds at Page 587 et seq., submitted the lands described therein to the form of ownership known as Condominium to be known and designated as Wharfside Manor Condominium No. I, and each of the aforesaid Master Deeds are referred to hereinafter collectively as "Master Deeds")

WHEREAS, in Section 9 of the Master Deeds, the Grantor has reserved the right to merge Wharfside Manor Condominium No. I and Wharfside Manor Condominium No. II into one Condominium to be known and designated as "Wharfside Manor Condominium"; and

WHEREAS, with the recordation of this Amendment, Grantor desires to effect the merger of said Condominium,

1. As provided in Section 9 of the Master Deeds, the Condominium known and designated as Wharfside Manor Condominium No. I and Wharfside Manor Condominium No. II are to be deemed merged as of the date this Amendment is recorded in the Office of the Registrar, of Deeds of Monmouth County, New Jersey.

2. The Condominium which is the product of the merger of Wharfside Manor Condominium No. I and Wharfside Manor Condominium No. II shall henceforth be known and designated as Wharfside Manor Condominium.

3. The legal description of Wharfside Manor Condominium shall be as set forth as Exhibit A to the Master Deeds and attached hereto as Exhibit A and depicted on Exhibit B to the Master Deeds and attached hereto as Exhibit B.

4. The percentage of interest of each condominium unit in the common elements of the Condominium is as set forth in Exhibit B to the Master Deeds and attached hereto as Exhibit C.

5. The Condominium shall be managed by the New Association as same is defined in Section 2(v) of the Master Deeds.

6. The easements and restrictions set forth in the Master Deeds shall, as provided in Section 5(C) of the Master Deeds, be deemed to have merged so as to provide for a uniform set of easements and restrictions for the Wharfside Manor Condominium.

7. The floor plans for the Condominium units shall remain unchanged.
