## Summary

Nick acquired a DJI Mini 4K drone and needed first-flight coaching. Conducted comprehensive research on setup, controls, regulations, and best practices. Created a mobile-friendly HTML cheat sheet for field reference.

## Key Findings

- DJI Mini 4K weighs 243g — under 250g FAA threshold, no registration required for recreational use
- TRUST test is required (free, online, ~30 min) — must carry proof while flying
- No internal storage — microSD card (U3/V30) is mandatory
- 31 min max flight time (real-world 25-27 min), 4K/30fps video, 3-axis gimbal
- O2 video link (older system) — 720p live feed, full 4K on card
- Gimbal protector MUST be removed before powering on (common mistake that damages motor)
- Beginner Mode caps altitude at 30m and limits speed — recommended for first flights
- Indoor flying strongly discouraged: no GPS lock, ATTI mode drift, prop wash turbulence
- Sub-250g drones exempt from Remote ID requirement
- QuickShots (Dronie, Circle, Helix, Rocket, Boomerang) are good for early cinematic footage
