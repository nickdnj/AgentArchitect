## Executive Summary

Nick DeMarco opened a T-Mobile for Business account on December 30, 2024 specifically to provide WiFi at the Wharfside pool area. This was a personal initiative — the account was opened in Nick's name via his personal email (nickd@demarconet.com) and then disclosed to management (Towne & Country Management at the time) for accounting purposes. The account appears to be a cellular data hotspot plan, not a fixed internet line.

Management requested a copy of any contract on December 30, 2024, and Nick replied on December 31, 2024 ("Here you go"), suggesting a contract or account confirmation was provided.

There is **no email evidence** of T-Mobile service stopping, cancellation discussions, or billing problems in the board inbox. However, a separate February 7, 2026 email from Giuseppe Gencarelli to Thomas Bopp explicitly flagged that the pool house phone service plan needs to be reviewed and that internet for the association room needs upgrading to high speed — suggesting the T-Mobile pool WiFi situation may be problematic or uncertain heading into 2026.

Separately, a major new WiFi project (Jack's Locksmith, LLC) was approved by the full board in December 2025 to install structured WiFi across Buildings 1, 4, and 7 with fob access — this is a different, larger project and does not replace the pool area T-Mobile service.
